About Festifys

Amsterdam, Netherlands · Founded 2025

Our Mission

Festifys was built for one reason: finding the right festival shouldn't be guesswork. We use your Spotify listening data to match you with festivals where you'll actually love the lineup.

How It Works

We compare your top 50 Spotify artists against the lineups of 35+ festivals across Europe and beyond. The result is a real match percentage based on who you actually listen to.

The Team

We're a small team of festival lovers and music nerds based in Amsterdam. We've been to Lowlands, Awakenings, Glastonbury and everything in between — and we built the tool we always wished existed.
